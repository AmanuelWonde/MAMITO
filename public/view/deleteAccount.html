How to Delete Your Account

Step 1: Go to the Settings Tab

Step 2: Tap on Delete Account

Step 3: Enter Your Password in the Dialog that Appears

Step 4: Tap on Delete

You have successfully deleted your account. Please note that you will no longer be able to log in with this account, and all your data associated with it will be permanently removed. If you wish to use our services again, you will need to create a new account.
